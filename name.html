Eklavya: my name

In the Devanagari script, my name is written as एकलव्य शर्मा.

To know how my name is pronounced, press the 'Listen' button on this Google Translate link.

In high school, my friends gave me the nickname eku (pronounced 'ay-koo'). You can call me by my nickname if you want.

I was named after a legendary archer who learned archery by self-study.
